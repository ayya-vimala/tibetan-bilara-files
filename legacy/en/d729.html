• D 729

The Noble

Dhāraṇī of Tārā

Homage to the Three Jewels!

namo ratna­trayāya | nama āryāvalokiteśvarāya bodhisattvāya mahā­sattvāya mahā­kāruṇikāya |

tad yathā | oṁ tāre tuttāre ture sarva­duṣṭa­praduṣṭān mama kṛte jambhaya stambhaya mohaya bandhaya hūṁ hūṁ hūṁ phaṭ phaṭ phaṭ sarva­duṣṭa­stambhani tāre svāhā |

This concludes “The Noble Dhāraṇī of Tārā.”
